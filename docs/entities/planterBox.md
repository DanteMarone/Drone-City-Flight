# Planter Box

## Overview
The Planter Box is a garden prop that adds color and greenery to outdoor spaces. It provides a compact wooden planter filled with animated flowers that sway softly to bring life to plazas, parks, and courtyards.

## Visuals
- **Wooden planter:** A box geometry wrapped with a procedural plank texture for a hand-built look.
- **Rim + soil:** A raised rim cap and dark soil insert create depth and contrast.
- **Flower cluster:** Multiple stems with spherical blooms arranged across the planter for variety.

## Key Parameters
- `width`: Overall planter width (default: `2.2`).
- `depth`: Overall planter depth (default: `0.9`).
- `height`: Overall planter height (default: `0.6`).

## Dependencies
- **Parent class:** `BaseEntity`.
- **Three.js primitives:** `BoxGeometry`, `CylinderGeometry`, `SphereGeometry`.
- **Materials:** `MeshStandardMaterial` with a procedural `CanvasTexture` for the wood planks.
